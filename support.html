About Solura

Solura is a lightweight, easy-to-use software platform for small shops and teams. It helps you manage staff rotas, track sales, maintain a customer database, and run simple monthly email campaigns — all from one dashboard.

Built for speed and simplicity, Solura is designed to reduce admin overhead so you can focus on running your business.
