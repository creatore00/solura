Privacy Policy

All data is securely stored and encrypted in transit using HTTPS. We do not share your information with third parties unless legally required.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Please contact support@solura.app to make a request.

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We'll notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
